What I actually do

I'm VigneshKumar Sivasamy — 3 years working in Identity & Access Management, currently managing Auth0 and Microsoft Entra ID while working closely with application developers to implement SSO across their applications.

That work has taught me a lot about how SSO actually works in practice — not just configuring a platform, but understanding the architecture behind a flow. Knowing when to recommend Authorization Code flow, when PKCE is required, when Client Credentials is the right choice, how tokens should be validated, what the signature check is actually doing and why it matters. That kind of decision-making is where most of my day-to-day knowledge lives.

Beyond SSO, I work with PowerShell for identity automation and have solid Power BI knowledge for reporting and visibility across identity data. I'm also genuinely interested in the broader ideas around Zero Trust and identity as a security layer — those concepts shape how I think about what I'm building, even when they're not something I'm implementing directly every day.

This site is where I document the real work — guides, fixes, and implementations that came from actually doing things, not just reading about them.
