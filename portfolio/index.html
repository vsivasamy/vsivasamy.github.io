Identity is

I'm VigneshKumar Sivasamy, a Jr. Software Engineer with around 4 years of experience specialising in Identity & Access Management. My core expertise spans Auth0, ADFS, and Microsoft Entra ID — with a strong focus on implementing SSO solutions across enterprise applications and managing Identity Providers at an organisational level, covering both workforce and customer identity use cases.

I bring solid cloud experience across AWS and Azure, and I'm comfortable automating identity infrastructure using Terraform — provisioning and managing Auth0 and Entra ID configurations through GitHub Actions pipelines. I believe in treating identity as code, not an afterthought.

I'm deeply passionate about the IAM and security space. That curiosity drives me to continuously explore new identity strategies, standards, and solutions — and this site is where I document that journey through real-world guides, fixes, and knowledge I've picked up along the way.
